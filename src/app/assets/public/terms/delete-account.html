Account Deletion

Follow these steps to permanently delete your Nightwave Plaza account.

  1. Sign in to your Nightwave Plaza account.
  2. Open My Profile.
  3. Go to Delete Account.
  4. Review the warning message.
  5. Confirm by selecting I understand, delete my account.
  6. Enter your current password if required.
  7. Click Delete Account.
Important
  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • All associated data will be deleted, including favorites and activity history.
  • You can register again using the same username and email if they are available.
